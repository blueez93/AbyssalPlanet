# Changelog

## 0.1.2

### Improvements

- Improved terrain transitions between Deep Abyssal, Abyssal Valley and Decay Road.
- Improved Georges Junior and Georges Senior companion movement.
- Added a status effect icon for Blue Gold Leaves.
- Reworked the Abyssal Hunter's Crossbow textures and loading states.
- Reworked placed Zwoing Flesh and Fresh Abyssal Meat models.

### Balance

- Reduced the experience granted by Abyssal Algae from 250 XP to 5 XP.

### Gameplay

- Dropped items now sink in the Abyssal dimension.
- Added a recipe for Blue Gold Saplings using three Abyssal Compost and one Blue Gold Leaves block.
- Explicitly linked Abyssal Vipers to their Fresh Abyssal Meat loot table.

### Fixes

- Fixed the Abyssal Hunter's Crossbow appearing disconnected in first person.
- Fixed bulky bait rendering when placed underwater.
- Reduced chunk-shaped biome and terrain boundaries.

## 0.1.1

- Initial public CurseForge release.
