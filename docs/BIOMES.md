# Biomes and Exploration

## Deep Abyssal

The central and darkest region. It contains Abyssal Octopuses, Mini-Krakens, luminous fish schools and the Kraken Boss. The Kraken is scheduled shortly after a player enters the dimension and later respawns after being defeated.

## Abyssal Valley

A ring of enormous sand dunes surrounding Deep Abyssal. It contains Zwoings, Blue Abyssal Moonfish, abyssal algae, exposed ore veins and rare Blue Gold trees on dunes that emerge above the water.

The Valley current pulls players toward Deep Abyssal. Holding the Totem of Abyssal Souls in the offhand prevents this current from affecting the player.

## Decay Road

The corrupted outer region. Its red water applies Toxic Burn to players, while creatures remain unaffected. The region contains the rare Abyssal Hunter's Crossbow and the entrance to the Abyssal Tunnel.

## Abyssal Tunnel

A unique, extremely deep cavern generated south of the arrival region. Georges-Briochard waits at its bottom alongside collectible Abyssal Eggs.
