# Getting Started

## Opening the Abyss

Craft a **Typhoon Caller** using a water bucket and four vanilla ink sacs. Place and activate it in the Overworld to create the entrance typhoon. The typhoon grows, removes water from its center and begins teleporting players who enter the drained area.

## First Arrival

Players arrive near the center of Deep Abyssal and temporarily receive Water Breathing, Dolphin's Grace and Night Vision. The arrival platform faces south, toward the outer regions and the eventual Abyssal Tunnel.

## Recommended Preparation

- Bring durable armor and food.
- Keep the Totem of Abyssal Souls in the offhand for underwater bonuses.
- Carry a Typhoon Caller before the first journey.
- Mark important locations and travel with another player when possible.
- Avoid attacking unfamiliar creatures until their behavior is understood.

## Returning

Entering the dimension grants the **Go Out** item. It returns the player near the original entrance typhoon and temporarily prevents immediate reabsorption.
