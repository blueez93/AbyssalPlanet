# Items and Equipment

## Totem of Abyssal Souls

Held in the offhand, the Totem grants Water Breathing, Night Vision, additional health and improved swimming. It also protects its bearer from the Abyssal Valley current.

## Abyssal Staff

A two-handed weapon powered by Abyssal Ink. It offers knockback in melee combat, charged ink projectiles and a cooperative portal ritual.

## Golden Blue Dagger

A fast Blue Gold weapon. Its special attacks consume Abyssal Toxin and apply stackable poison through independent main-hand and offhand charges.

## Abyssal Hunter's Crossbow

A rare weapon discovered on the floor of Decay Road. It behaves like a vanilla crossbow, applies a stacking toxic burn and transforms every fifth shot into a delayed high-speed projectile.

## Abyssal Pouch

A thirty-slot storage item linked to a temporary ghostly copy that other players can open remotely. Each use of the ghostly copy permanently reduces its capacity.

## Abyssal Soup

A multiplayer teleportation meal made from Abyssal Tentacles. It connects two players through a dangerous shared health and regeneration sequence.

## Blue Gold

Blue Gold Ore is found throughout the dimension and is especially visible in Abyssal Valley. It can be smelted into ingots and used for equipment. Blue Gold leaves provide healing or temporary additional hearts.
