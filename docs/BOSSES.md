# Bosses

## Kraken Boss

The Kraken appears in Deep Abyssal after a player enters the dimension. Before combat it circles its prey for roughly twenty seconds. It then begins the encounter with a rapid intimidation charge and reveals its boss bar.

During combat it uses tentacle strikes at close range, ink projectiles from a distance and additional charges to reposition. Only one Kraken Boss can be active in the dimension.

## Abyssal Serpent

Place Zwoing Flesh to attract an Abyssal Viper. Its fresh meat can then be used as a second bait. Another Viper arrives, and the Abyssal Serpent emerges to kill it before engaging nearby players.

The Serpent charges for a percentage of the target's health and can only lose a limited amount of health per attack. Its venom can permanently enhance eligible weapons.

## Georges-Briochard

Georges-Briochard guards the bottom of the Abyssal Tunnel. It is peaceful until attacked. Once hostile, it bites and latches onto one player, applying stacking Bleeding until another player forces it to release its victim.

Defeating Georges rewards its placeable and wearable head trophy.
