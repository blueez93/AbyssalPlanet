# Creatures and Companions

## Abyssal Wildlife

- **Abyssal Octopus**: defensive creature that attacks when threatened and releases poisonous ink.
- **Mini-Kraken**: a dangerous Deep Abyssal predator.
- **Luminous Abyssal Fish**: schooling fish that illuminate parts of Deep Abyssal.
- **Blue Abyssal Moonfish**: Valley fish that can provide useful skin.
- **Zwoing**: small collectible bottom-dwellers with several rarity-based colors.
- **Abyssal Viper**: summoned by placing Zwoing Flesh as bait.

## Baby Kraken

A very rare companion found in Abyssal Valley, or in Deep Abyssal while the Kraken Boss is active. It can be tamed and healed with Abyssal Compost, follows its owner across dimensions and defends them with ink and bubble attacks.

## Zwoings

Zwoings can be picked up, placed or thrown. Thrown Zwoings attach to targets and apply different utility effects. Combining all color variants creates a stronger multicolor Zwoing.

## Georges Junior and Senior

Abyssal Eggs may hatch into Georges Junior. Some Juniors can grow into Georges Senior, a large saddleable aquatic mount. A mounted Senior grants Water Breathing and Night Vision and can perform a powerful bite.

## Abyssal Shrimp

An exceptionally rare companion hidden among the possible Abyssal Egg outcomes.
